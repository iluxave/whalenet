Instructions

Draw outline of a whale tail

  • Please draw a contour using the mouse left button to click and connect points that outline whale tail in maximum detail.
  • To finish the contour polygon use the mouse right button. This command will connect the first to the last point.
  • Remove the last point by clicking Ctrl + mouse click or click on the Undo button.
  • Once polygon is closed, you may move points around by clicking and dragging
  • It is also possible to add new points to a closed polygon, by using Shift+left mouse button
  • Clear all points by clicking on the Reset button
  • In order to get accepted, the polygon must match the tail outline as close as possible, including extrusions and notches
  • Various growths and attachments are also considered to be part of the tail
  • Use as many points as needed - a good contour will usually have a few hundred
  • Use browser's zoom-in functionality to check that your contour matches the whale tail well
